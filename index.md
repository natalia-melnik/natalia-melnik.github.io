







I am a PhD candidate at the [Psychophysics of Appearance Laboratory](http://appearancelab.org/) headed by Prof. Bilge Sayim at the [Institute of Psychology](https://www.psy.unibe.ch/), [University of Bern](https://www.unibe.ch/) (CH). I use psychophysics and appearance-based methods to investigate shape perception in the periphery. I received a M.Sc. in Cognitive Science in 2015. For my master's project I used EEG to investigate the effect of semantic interference in an auditory short-term memory task.
