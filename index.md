

I am a PhD candidate working at the [Psychophysics of Appearance Laboratory](http://appearancelab.org/), a research laboratory at the [Institute of Psychology](https://www.psy.unibe.ch/), [University of Bern](https://www.unibe.ch/) (CH). I am fascinated about peripheral vision! My research is focused on understanding shape and object perception in the periphery by using psychophysics and appearance-based methods. 

I received a M.Sc. in Cognitive Science in 2015. For my master's project I used EEG to investigate the effect of semantic interference in an auditory short-term memory task.
